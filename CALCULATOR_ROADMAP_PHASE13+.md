## Phase 13: Tradespeople & Services (8 Calculators) — NEW CATEGORY

**Why this category?** Zero competition from existing calculator sites. Tradespeople search "how to price a job" constantly but find only blog posts, not interactive tools. High commercial intent (people pricing real work). Strong word-of-mouth potential in trade communities.

### 13.1 Job Quote Calculator
**Target Keyword:** "job quote calculator for tradesmen", "how to price a job"
**Inputs:** Trade type (plumber/electrician/builder/painter/general), materials list with costs, labour hours, hourly rate, markup %, VAT rate, travel/callout fee
**Outputs:** Total quote, materials subtotal, labour subtotal, markup amount, VAT amount, profit margin, printable quote summary
**Unique Angle:** Trade-specific presets (plumber hourly rate differs from electrician). Generates a professional-looking quote breakdown the tradesperson can show clients.
**Color Theme:** Slate
**Difficulty:** Medium
**SEO Rationale:** "how to quote a job" gets consistent search volume from new tradespeople. No good interactive tools exist — just blog posts with rules of thumb.

### 13.2 Tradesperson Hourly Rate Calculator
**Target Keyword:** "what to charge per hour as a tradesman", "plumber hourly rate calculator"
**Inputs:** Desired annual income, working weeks/year, billable hours/week, overheads (van, insurance, tools, training), tax rate
**Outputs:** Required hourly rate, day rate, effective take-home, overhead breakdown, comparison to industry averages
**Unique Angle:** Works backwards from desired income. Shows how many non-billable hours (quoting, travel, admin) eat into the rate.
**Color Theme:** Orange
**Difficulty:** Easy
**SEO Rationale:** Every new tradesperson asks this question. Seasonal spike when people start their own business in January.

### 13.3 Materials Markup Calculator
**Target Keyword:** "how much to mark up materials", "materials markup calculator"
**Inputs:** Cost price, desired markup % OR desired selling price, quantity, trade category
**Outputs:** Sell price, profit per unit, total profit, margin % vs markup % (explains the difference)
**Unique Angle:** Common mistake explainer: margin vs markup (50% markup = 33% margin). Trade-specific typical markups.
**Color Theme:** Amber
**Difficulty:** Easy
**SEO Rationale:** "markup vs margin" is a perennial confusion. Calculator + explainer captures both informational and transactional intent.

### 13.4 Painting & Decorating Quote Calculator
**Target Keyword:** "painting cost calculator per room", "how much to charge for painting a room"
**Inputs:** Room dimensions (or presets: small/medium/large), number of coats, paint type (emulsion/gloss/primer), prep work needed, ceiling included
**Outputs:** Paint quantity needed, paint cost, labour hours, total quote, price per sqm
**Unique Angle:** Dual-use: homeowners estimating costs AND painters pricing jobs. Includes prep time estimates (filling, sanding, masking).
**Color Theme:** Cream
**Difficulty:** Easy
**SEO Rationale:** "how much does it cost to paint a room" is high volume and seasonal (spring/summer). Captures both consumer and trade searches.

### 13.5 Landscaping Quote Calculator

### 13.6 Roofing Cost Calculator
