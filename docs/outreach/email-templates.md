149 free online calculators for finance, tax, health, and everyday decisions.

### Description (100 words)

All calculators are also available as free embeddable widgets for bloggers and publishers.

- 149 calculators across 10+ categories

- No signup, no email capture, no paywall
- Mobile-friendly, fast loading (Lighthouse 95+)
- Free embeddable widgets for any website
- UK and US tax calculators (income tax, self-employment, student loans)
- Financial planning tools (FIRE, pension, mortgage, compound interest)
- Lifestyle calculators (BBQ, paint, flooring, brewing, candle making)

### Target Audience

Anyone who Googles "how much will X cost" or "how do I calculate Y." Primary audiences: UK and US adults making financial decisions, homeowners, freelancers, and personal finance enthusiasts.

### Pricing

## Usage Notes

- Always personalise [PLACEHOLDER] fields before sending.
- For Template 1, include a link to the specific embed preview page, not just the main site.
- For Template 3, research the target company first. The [SPECIFIC_REASON] line is what makes this work. Generic pitches get ignored.
- Follow up once after 5-7 days if no reply. After that, move on.
- Track all outreach in a spreadsheet: date sent, contact, site, template used, response, outcome.
